Another interesting thing about the VS cluster F cams is that they try to hide the config params, e.g. there is no .ini or .conf files in the /system directory, only the ./www sound and the ./modules files. This is the 10.18 series, e.g. 10.18.121.17 and 10.18.123.10, etc. they seem to write their config info directly to the mtd5 block:

[root@procam2:~]# cat /proc/mtd dev: size erasesize name mtd0: 00040000 00001000 "boot" mtd1: 000d0000 00001000 "tag" mtd2: 001f0000 00008000 "kernel" mtd3: 00460000 00008000 "rootfs" mtd4: 00060000 00008000 "system" mtd5: 00040000 00008000 "param" mtd6: 00800000 00008000 "all"

And just like you state, they do all sorts of voodoo with their firmware files, which binwalk can't handle (my version, anyway), it only extracts 757EAC.zlib which I believe is the root fs:
