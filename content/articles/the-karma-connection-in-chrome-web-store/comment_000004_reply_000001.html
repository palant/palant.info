I don’t think there is an easy way to remove specifically affiliate cookies. On the bright side, they typically expire within a few days anyway, even if you don’t delete your cookies.

Your browsing history is more problematic unfortunately. You have to assume that somebody has been watching over your shoulder for the past year, noting the address of every website you’ve visited. Typically, that’s enough data to link various of your online identities and email addresses to your name and sometimes even your physical address. And it’s all of your browsing habits in there, often also people you communicate with.

If you are in the EU, you can try to exercise your rights under GDPR in order to make Karma Shopping Ltd. remove your data. The success chances are rather slim unfortunately, given that they likely already sold this data to some number of third parties that they have no control over. In the typical case, you now have to live with the fact that some advertising companies know a lot more about you than they should. A quickly growing group of people have other threat scenarios however and need to consider their chances of being persecuted should their government get its hands on this data.
